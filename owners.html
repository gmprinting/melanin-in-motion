Meet the Founders

Melanin In Motion is a culture-driven dance brand founded on January 26, 2025, by Temeshia and Justin Harris. What started with our first-ever line dance has grown into a movement celebrating confidence, culture, and community. Our merchandise is designed to represent rhythm, pride, and unity—made for those who move with purpose and wear their culture boldly.

Our Why

We believe movement can change how you see yourself. Not just on the dance floor, but in everyday life. Melanin In Motion was built to be a place where you can learn, laugh, and level up, one count at a time.

Our Mission

Melanin In Motion exists to build culture-driven, confidence-growing spaces where people feel seen, supported, and celebrated. We use line dance as the spark, but the mission is bigger: create community, keep it beginner-friendly, and make every event feel like a safe place to show up as you are. Whether you’re brand new, returning after a break, or you’ve been stepping for years, you belong here.

Our Promise

We promise clarity, consistency, and good energy. You’ll get step-by-step teaching, patient breakdowns, and real encouragement without pressure or judgment. We’ll communicate class details clearly, keep the vibe respectful, and protect the culture of the room so everyone can learn, have fun, and grow. If you ever feel lost, we’ll meet you where you are and get you back on beat.

What to Expect

Expect a welcoming atmosphere, structured instruction, and music that matches the moment. Classes start with a warm-up and simple foundations, then we build into combos and full dances at a pace that makes sense. At events, expect trail ride energy when it’s time to turn up, plus plenty of chances to connect, take videos, and celebrate progress. Bring water, wear comfortable shoes, and come ready to move at your level. The only requirement is effort and respect.
